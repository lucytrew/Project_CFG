Dehydration

Dehydration occurs when your body loses more fluid than you take in. Dehydration can also lead to a loss of strength and stamina. It's a main cause of heat exhaustion. You should be able to reverse dehydration at this stage by drinking more fluids. If dehydration is ongoing (chronic), it can affect your kidney function and increase the risk of kidney stones. It can also lead to muscle damage and constipation.

Symptoms include:

Thirst

Tiredness
